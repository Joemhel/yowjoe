Joemhel Coral is a Computer Engineering student at the University of the East in Manila, Philippines. His obsession with drawing illustrations begins in the slums of Veronica Street in Gulod, Quezon City. The kid enjoys child-like drawings, which reflect his artistic journey since childhood. He was able to make a living from his art through commissions and hoped to one day have his own illustrated book.

He loves minimalism not because he is into clean style, the kid sucks for over-the-top details. That's why.
